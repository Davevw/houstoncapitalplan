A retail-forward concept built around a destination restaurant group interest. High-visibility corner lots on Bissonnet reserved for restaurant pads with outdoor dining. Fuel/convenience anchor on Lot 8. Neighborhood retail and professional services along frontage lots 19-22. Multifamily scaled to two lots (15-16) positioned adjacent to retail for walkable foot traffic. Flex district allows showroom on Lot 9 closest to retail, with pure warehouse on interior lots 6-7. Light industrial confined to lots 10-12 and 18.
